ELIZA

A recreation of the 1966 MIT chatbot — the first time humans fell in love with a machine.

Joseph Weizenbaum built ELIZA at MIT in 1966. He was disturbed by how quickly people formed emotional bonds with it — including his own secretary, who asked him to leave the room so she could speak with it privately. He spent the rest of his life warning us about this moment.

Original ELIZA script preserved and shared by Charles Hayden.
